When should you opt for wood shake siding?

If you are going for the rustic and modern look at your house, you should check out wood shake siding. This siding is made of wood, usually cedar, and covers the exterior of your house. It gives a modern yet very cozy look to your home. It has become a popular choice for many in recent decades.

Benefits of wood shake siding

This is a great choice for many homeowners, as it is perfectly paired up with wood shingles or a cedar wood roof. It will give you a more earthy and close-to-nature feel and make your house look welcoming.
